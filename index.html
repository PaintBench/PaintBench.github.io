PaintBench

Easy tasks in a raster editor, still out of reach for frontier image editing models.

  • 20 fundamental precise visual editing operations with a single correct anwer.
  • Procedural generation enables infinite problem variety, protecting against contamination.
  • Deterministic pixel-level evaluation. No reliance on bias-prone judge models.
  • Precise per-task breakdown and adjustable difficulty reveals individual model strengths and weaknesses.

The highest-performing model we evaluated (Nano Banana 2) scores only 17.1%.

We also built TinyGrafixBench

Can model performance on PaintBench generalize to data visualization editing? To find out, we created TinyGrafixBench: a procedurally generated, deterministically evaluated benchmark of 20 such tasks spanning 5 chart types.

Findings: model rankings strongly correlate between PaintBench and TinyGrafixBench, suggesting that PaintBench performance may generalize to other tasks built upon similar fundamental operations.
